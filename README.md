# Business-Strategy-Analysis-of-Zepto
A comprehensive business analysis of Zepto using qualitative and quantitative research, including market analysis, customer sentiment, competitor benchmarking, SWOT analysis, and strategic business recommendations.

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive business strategy analysis of **Zepto**, one of India's leading quick-commerce startups. The study combines qualitative and quantitative research methods to evaluate Zepto's business model, customer perception, competitive landscape, strengths, challenges, and future growth opportunities.

The objective is to provide actionable business recommendations based on market research, customer feedback, and strategic analysis.

---

## Business Problem

Quick-commerce companies are experiencing rapid growth while facing increasing competition, evolving customer expectations, rising operational costs, and pressure to achieve profitability.

This project analyzes Zepto's current market position and identifies strategic opportunities to improve customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and long-term business growth.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze Zepto's business model
- Study market position and industry trends
- Evaluate customer perception using sentiment analysis

## Key Business Insights

- Zepto's biggest competitive advantage is its ultra-fast delivery model.
- Strong dark-store infrastructure enables efficient operations.
- Customer satisfaction is driven primarily by speed and convenience.
- High operational costs remain one of the company's biggest challenges.
- Customer feedback indicates opportunities to improve customer support and inventory availability.
- Expansion into Tier-2 cities and private-label products can drive future growth.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### Short-Term

- Improve inventory forecasting
- Enhance customer support
- Reduce delivery charges for loyal customers
- Increase product availability

### Long-Term

- Expand into Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities
- Strengthen private-label offerings
- Enhance subscription programs
- Continue investing in AI-driven logistics and demand forecasting
